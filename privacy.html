Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2025

This application is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal data during the use of our app.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of data from users:

  • Email address: Used for account authentication and support communication.
  • Phone number: Used for user verification, technical support, and consultation.
  • Photos & videos: Used to capture product images or select from the device gallery.
  • Files and storage: Used to store product images, invoices, or other related data.
  • Location: Required for scanning and connecting to Bluetooth devices (such as printers) as mandated by the Android system.
  • Device identifiers: Used for push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).

2. Purpose of Data Use

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating user accounts (email and phone number).
  • Supporting app functionality such as barcode scanning, printing invoices, and capturing product images.
  • Sending order status updates or operational notifications via FCM.

3. How Data is Collected

We collect data when:

  • The user registers an account and provides personal information.
  • The user interacts with the app (e.g., barcode scanning, photo capture, invoice printing, Bluetooth connection).
  • The app receives device identifiers from Firebase for push notification purposes.

4. Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

We use Google’s Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) service to deliver push notifications to user devices. FCM collects device identifiers to enable message delivery.

This information is used solely for technical purposes and is not shared with any third parties beyond Firebase (Google).

5. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Data is shared with Firebase for push notification services.
  • When required by law or government authorities.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and administrative measures to ensure that personal data is secure and protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of their personal data by contacting us via the information below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be clearly announced within the app or on our website.
